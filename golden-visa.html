Golden Visa

Al Barq Documents Clearing Service provides Golden Visa Services, offering a long-term residence permit allowing foreign nationals to reside, work, or study in the United Arab Emirates while enjoying special benefits. Investors, business owners, scientists, students and graduates, front-line heroes, and humanitarian pioneers are eligible for the Golden visa. This initiative by His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President and Prime Minister of the UAE, aims to make the UAE one of the most investor-friendly nations globally. Holders of long-term visas are permitted to remain outside the country for as long as they wish, freely entering and exiting at their convenience. They are exempted from the rule stating that residents cannot be outside the UAE for more than six consecutive months.
