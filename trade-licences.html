Trade Licenses

Al Barq Documents Clearing Service is poised to assist you in obtaining Trade Licenses for your business setup in Ras Al Khaimah. The process of securing a trade license in RAK involves navigating through numerous procedures, laws, and requirements. Before seeking permission from the Department of Economic Development (DED), you must first select a trade name and provide the necessary memoranda and articles. Errors commonly occur during this process. However, with the guidance of Al Barq's trade license services, obtaining a trade license in Ras Al Khaimah can be achieved swiftly, effortlessly, and transparently.
    Following the formation of a company in RAK, acquiring a trade license is the most crucial step. This authorization allows the company to conduct operations throughout the emirate. The Ras Al Khaimah government's initiatives to promote the establishment of companies have expanded, presenting opportunities for numerous new startups and business owners to establish their enterprises in the emirate.
